Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 31, 2026

Overview

Campus Vault is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This page explains the types of information we collect, how we use it, how to request deletion, and contact information.

Information we collect

To provide you with the best experience, we may collect the following data:

  • Account Information: Basic details such as your email and username when you sign up.
  • User Content: Study materials, notes, or comments you voluntarily upload or post.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data to help us improve service performance and user experience.

How we use information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Campus Vault platform.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • For security purposes and legal compliance.

Data sharing

We value your trust. We do not sell personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers that support our operations (such as hosting or database services), strictly under contract and for limited use.

Your choices

You have control over your data. You can request the deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us directly.

Email Privacy Team noreply.campusvault@gmail.com

We will respond to all valid requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated effective date.
