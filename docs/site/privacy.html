Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-31

Overview

Campus Vault is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This page explains the types of information we collect, how we use it, how to request deletion, and contact information.

Information we collect

  • Account information (email, username) when you sign up.
  • Content you upload or post.
  • Analytics data for improving service (aggregated and anonymized where possible).

How we use information

We use information to operate, maintain, and improve the service; provide support; and for security and legal compliance.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers that support our operations, under contract and limited use.

Your choices

You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting us at privacy@campusvault.app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes

We may update this policy; we’ll post changes here with an updated effective date.

Questions? Contact privacy@campusvault.app
